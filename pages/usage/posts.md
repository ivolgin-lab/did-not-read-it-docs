# Submitting Posts

Posts always belong to exactly one didnotreadit. There are two kinds:

- **Text post** — a title and a body of text. Use this for discussions, questions, and write-ups.
- **Link post** — a title and an external URL. Use this to share an article, video, or any other off-instance resource.

## Submit a post

1. Sign in.
2. Go to the didnotreadit you want to post in: `/d/<name>`.
3. Click **submit** (or go directly to `/d/<name>/submit`).
4. Choose **text** or **link** at the top of the form.
5. Fill in the title (up to 300 characters) and either the body (text post) or URL (link post).
6. Submit.

You'll be redirected to the new post.

## Title guidance

The title is the only thing most people will read, so make it count.

- For link posts, prefer the article's actual title over editorialized commentary.
- For text posts, summarize the question or claim — don't make readers click to find out what you mean.

## Edit and delete

Posts you authored show edit and delete controls when you're signed in as that user. Edits update the body but preserve the score and comment count. Deletes are permanent.

## Voting on your own posts

Your own posts start with an automatic upvote from you. You can remove it, but most people leave it.
