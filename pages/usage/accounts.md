# Accounts

You can browse posts and comments without an account, but you need to sign in to vote, submit posts, comment, or create a didnotreadit.

## Register

1. Click **register** in the header (or go to `/register`).
2. Pick a **username** (3–20 characters, must be unique on the instance).
3. Choose a **password**.
4. Submit. You'll be signed in immediately.

Usernames are permanent — they appear next to every post and comment you make and can't be changed later.

## Log in

Click **login** in the header (or go to `/login`), enter your username and password, and submit. Sessions persist across browser restarts until you log out.

## Log out

Click **logout** in the header. This clears your session on this device only.

## Your profile

Every user has a public profile at `/user/<username>` listing the posts and comments they've made. Click your own username in the header to jump to yours.

## Forgotten passwords

There is currently no self-service password reset. If you forget your password, contact the instance administrator.
