# Comments

Comments are how discussions happen. Every post has its own comment thread on the post page (`/post/<id>`).

## Reply to a post

1. Sign in.
2. Open the post.
3. Type into the comment box at the top of the comment section and submit.

Your comment appears at the top level of the thread.

## Reply to a comment

Each comment has a **reply** link beneath it. Click it to open an inline reply box. Submitting threads your reply directly under that comment, indented one level. There is no hard limit on nesting depth — threads can go as deep as the conversation needs.

## Voting on comments

Just like posts, every comment has up- and down-arrow buttons. Comments are sorted by score, so the highest-voted replies float to the top of each thread level.

## Edit and delete

You can edit or delete comments you authored. Edits replace the body. Deletes are permanent and will leave child replies orphaned but still visible (with the deleted comment shown as `[deleted]`).

## Etiquette

- Reply at the right level. If you're answering a specific point, reply to *that* comment, not to the post.
- Quote sparingly. The parent comment is right above yours.
- Downvote things that don't belong, not things you disagree with.
