# Browsing and Voting

## The "all" feed

`/d/all` is the front page. It lists the newest posts from every didnotreadit on the instance, paginated. This is where `/` sends you by default.

## A single didnotreadit

`/d/<name>` shows posts from one community only — for example, `/d/announcements`. The page header shows the didnotreadit's title, description, and creator.

## Discovering didnotreadits

`/d/new` lists all didnotreadits on the instance, sorted by creation date. Use this to find communities to follow or to check whether one already exists before creating your own.

## A single post

Click any post title to open it at `/post/<id>`. This page shows the full body (for text posts) or the link (for link posts), the score, and the full comment thread.

## Voting

Each post and comment has up- and down-arrow buttons next to it. To vote, you need to be signed in.

- **Upvote** — click the up arrow. Click again to remove your vote.
- **Downvote** — click the down arrow. Click again to remove your vote.
- Switching between up and down replaces your existing vote — you only ever count once per item.

The score updates immediately. Posts and comments are sorted by score within their context, so a heavily upvoted comment floats to the top of a thread.
